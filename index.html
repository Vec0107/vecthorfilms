About

A Filmmaker Driven by Story

I am a documentary filmmaker and visual storyteller based in the United Kingdom, originally from Nigeria. My work sits at the intersection of personal experience and universal truth — finding the extraordinary within ordinary lives.

Through long-form documentaries, short films, and cinematic portraits, I explore identity, community, and belonging. I am actively building a body of work within the UK screen industry, driven by the belief that authentic stories — told with care and craft — can change how people see each other.

Director’s Statement

Why I Make Films

Every frame is a choice. Every story is a responsibility. I make films because silence is never neutral.

I came to filmmaking through a need to document what I witnessed — communities often overlooked, voices rarely amplified, lives lived in full colour but recorded in monochrome. Growing up in Nigeria and now building a career in the UK, I carry two worlds in my lens. That tension, that richness, is the engine of my work.

My documentaries are character-led. I believe in long observation, earned trust, and the moment when a subject forgets the camera is there. That is where truth lives. Whether I am filming a community celebration, a personal journey, or a social portrait, I am always asking: what does this person’s life reveal about all of us?

I am applying for the Global Talent Visa as part of a deliberate, long-term commitment to the UK screen industry. I want to make films here, collaborate here, and contribute a perspective that is genuinely underrepresented in British documentary filmmaking.

Practice & Focus

The Work I Take On

I take on a limited number of projects each year to ensure every film receives the depth, time, and intention it deserves. If your project fits the spirit of any of these, I would like to hear from you.

01
Documentary Film
Long and short-form documentary production from development through to final cut. Character-led, observational, and socially engaged work. Available for commission, co-production, and collaborative development.
Open to commission · Co-production welcome · Let’s begin with a conversation.
02
Cinematic Portraits & Community Stories
Cultural events, community celebrations, and personal portraits filmed with documentary sensitivity and craft. I do not cover events — I find the story inside them.
Available for community organisations, cultural bodies, and arts institutions.
03
Brand & Institutional Film
Story-driven films for organisations whose work deserves to be seen clearly and felt deeply. Not promotional content — films that earn the viewer’s attention.
For organisations aligned with social, cultural, or creative missions.
