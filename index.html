About VecthorFilms

Crafting Cinematic Memories

Based in the UK and originally from Nigeria, I bring a rich cultural perspective to every project. With a background rooted in storytelling and a passion for visual art, VecthorFilms was born from the belief that every event deserves to be remembered exactly as it felt.

Specialising in weddings and community events, I work with couples and organisations across the UK to deliver polished, emotional, and timeless video content that goes beyond simple documentation.
